For a mostly read-only portfolio and blog, a **static generator** plus **GitHub Pages** keeps deploys predictable: one pipeline, no runtime database, and no separate CMS app to maintain.

When you need interactivity—like comments—**embedded widgets** or a managed service often beat a custom auth + comments stack for a personal site.
